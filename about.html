About Me

Back in 2019, while I was building my startups, I truly believed I had what it takes to be an entrepreneur:

The zeal.
The enthusiasm.
The hustle.

But sometimes, things don’t go as planned.
COVID hit.
Dreams took a backseat.
The financial pressure grew.

Still, amidst the chaos, I was recognised as one of the Top 50 Global Marketing Leaders at MADCON Dubai, 2019.

And as always, I came out stronger. One thing that stayed with me throughout? Persistence.

In 2022, I stepped into the 9-to-5 world, rebuilt from scratch, and sharpened my skills. In 2024, I was honoured with the Young Achiever’s Award.

Also completed my Sales & Marketing specialization from IIM Indore, and continue to support the Customer Success community actively.
From building companies, doing sales and marketing, to even coding—I've worn many hats.

This website is a reflection of that journey.

Thanks for stopping by. Let’s connect and have a conversation. And hey, if I’m not working—you’ll probably find me creating content for my bike page.

Give it a follow—you just might enjoy it!
