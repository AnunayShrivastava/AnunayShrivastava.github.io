Welcome!

Hi, I’m Anunay. From building an SAE/BAJA F2 car prototype at 20 to launching three startups by 24, my journey has been anything but linear. After the pandemic, I pivoted to Customer Success—and found my stride at the crossroads of business, tech, and people. Whether it’s sales, marketing, coding, or community building—I’ve done it all, and I’m still learning. My work has been featured in global publications, but what drives me is creating an impact, both on and off the track. Beyond growth numbers and awards, I lead communities, push limits on a bike, travel with purpose, and empower others to do the same. Always building. Always evolving.
